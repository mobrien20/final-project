who we are

Our names our Madison, Rizza, Meaghan. We decided to dedicate our final project to endangered spieces of the world because we know this is a pressing global crisis. Each year, more and more animals are added to the International Union for Conservation of Nature’s Red List of Threatened Species. This issue needs global attention which is why we are using our gained coding knowledge to raise awareness so people are cognisant about the animals that need our help.

why we care

Imagine your home, food, and mental state dwindling everyday, except there is nothing you can do to fix it. That is what is happening to the endangered spieces in the world. We care because animals are living creatures just like us and deserve the same respect, acknowlegdment, and happiness that humans do. Show your support by donating to one of the organisations below. The world and the animals need our help.
