About

An endangered species is a group of organisms threated by extinction. There are over 1,400 endangered species listed by the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Services. From the Blue Whale to the African Elephants, endangered species will become extinct as demonstrated by the Bald Eagles. Too many days go by where this animal crisis goes unnoticed. The Endangered World aims to provide awareness to this global issue and explain why these species are suffering in the way they are.
