You Can Help


Ways to save the animals and help slow down climate change:


  • use less (or don't buy) plastic items (straws, bags, cups, etc.,

  • donate to charities,

  • make charities,

  • spread the word,

  • reduce, reuse and recycle,

  • volunteer to do public clean ups or protecting the wildlife,

  • never shop in the Black Market,

  • prevent the use of herbicides and pesticides,

  • ride a bike, take a public transit,

  • carpool or use a fuel-efficient car,

  • use renewable energy,

  • pull plugs that are not in use,

  • avoid products with a lot of packaging,

  • never buy animals skins/furs,

  • plant a tree.
